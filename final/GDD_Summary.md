## 01 Day / Night Cycle

### ☀️ Day Phase — 7 AM – 7 PM

- 🌾 Farm crops, water the garden, harvest
- ⛏ Mine resources (caves, ruins)
- 🏗 Build, upgrade, craft
- 🍳 Cook food, craft Bottles at the Crafting Station
- 💰 Sell at the Farm Market
- 🐔 Feed & check the Coop
- 📦 Open Treasure Pods on the map

### 🌙 Night Phase — 7 PM – 3 AM

- ⚔️ Monster waves attack your base
- 🔫 Use Crop Turret + player weapons to defend
- 💰 Collect Granumz & resource drops from kills
- 🌿 Night farming is possible — caves/ruins stay accessible
- ⚠️ Higher risk but richer loot in deeper zones at night

### 💤 Sleep Phase

- 😴 Can sleep right after clearing each night's threat, or from 3 AM onward
- ⚙️ Machines and structures continue running automatically while the player sleeps
- ⏳ Sleeping early = losing night farming minutes. If you've optimized your time and got everything sorted on the farm — get some sleep.

> **Sleep Buff:** Fully restore HP & Energy on wakeup. +25% movement speed in the morning (7–10 AM)

## 02 Core Loop

```
🌾 Farm → ⚗️ Process → 🎒 Prepare → ⚔️ Fight → 🏗️ Upgrade
```

**Farm:** Mine resources, prepare energy, filter water, plant and harvest crops

**Process:** Process ingredients, cook food, craft Bottles

**Prepare:** Select the right ammo and Bottle, load up the Crop Turret

**Fight:** Eliminate monster waves, collect Granumz & other drops

**Upgrade:** Purchase blueprints, build & upgrade structures

### Ammo = Crops Principle

> 1 harvest = 1 ammo = 1 crop. Bundle crops (Wheat): 1 harvest = 1 bundle = n crops.

Each crop can be used as:
- 🔫 Ammo
- ⚗️ Base ingredient for other items (e.g., Coconut → Coconut Milk)
- 🧪 Ingredient for crafting Bottles
- 🍞 Ingredient for cooking food
- 💰 Sold at the Farm Market for Granumz
- 🐔 Chicken feed

## 05 Recipes — Crafting Station

## Recipe Slot Rules (3 slots)

- Quy tắc ingame: **Mọi recipe luôn phải có đúng 3 slot** (3 cụm `Item ×N`).
- Nếu recipe chỉ có **2 thành phần** `A×m + B×n`, hãy **tách 1 thành phần** thành 2 slot để đủ 3:
    - `A×m + B×n` → `A×a + B×n + A×(m−a)` (với `1 ≤ a < m`)
- Ưu tiên tách thành phần có số lượng lớn hơn / dễ chia hơn.
- Ví dụ:
    - `🍇 Grape ×16 + 🍬 Sugar ×1` → `🍇 Grape ×8 + 🍬 Sugar ×1 + 🍇 Grape ×8`
    - `🛢️ Bean Oil ×3 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1` → `🛢️ Bean Oil ×2 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 + 🛢️ Bean Oil ×1`
    - `🧪 Vial Lv1 ×2 + 💎 Ruby ×2` → `🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 + 💎 Ruby ×2 + 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1` (hoặc tách Ruby tương tự)

## 07 Coop — Hen & Rooster

### Chicken Lifecycle

```
🐔 Hen/Rooster (6 breeding cycles)
    → 🥚 Lays Egg (every 12h in-game)
    → 🐣 Hatches in 24h (50/50 Hen · Rooster)
```

After 6 breeding cycles:
- ⚠️ Overwork → only 50% meat
- 📉 CL Penalty → -1 cycle + CL/batch
- 🍖 Normal Meat *(WIP)*

**Key stats:**
- Eggs per batch = random(1–3) × count of majority side
- Feed: 🌾 200 Wheat / chicken / day
- Hatch rate: 50/50, self-adjusts ±5% per hatch
- **Recommendation:** keep imbalance (CL) = 0 to avoid overwork

Central item storage vault. ⚠ Deposit only — items cannot be retrieved. Connect with Refinery Link to deposit remotely.

### 🏪 Builder's Shop

Purchase blueprints with Granumz and materials. Some special blueprints are only available from Treasure Pods (level 3 Gadgets). Purchased blueprints are used in Fabricate Gadget.

### 🔧 Fabricate Gadget

Craft Gadgets from purchased blueprints. Holds 1 Gadget at a time. Flow: Press Fabricate → move to map → select designated location → place.

### 💰 Farm Market

Sell crops and items for Granumz — the main currency. Dynamic pricing system: Perlin Noise + quantity-sold variance. Selling too much of one type → price drops. Diversify to maintain good prices. Connect Market Link to sell remotely.

| # | Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---------|--------|
| 1 | Sell Value +5% (round 1) | Blueprint purchased at Builder's Shop |
| 2 | Sell Value +5% (round 2) | Total max +10% sell value (Unlock via Unlock Events) |

### Gadgets (crafted via Fabricate Gadget)

**⛏ EXTRACTORS** — Automatically extract resources. 3 levels per gadget.

- ⛏️ **Drill** — Extracts resources
- 💧 **Pump** — Pumps water into Pond/Refinery
- 🐝 **Apiary** — Auto-collects honey *(Trigger: 🤖 Place first Apiary)*

**🔧 UTILITIES**

- 🔫 **Crop Turret** — Load crops → auto-shoots monsters at night. Upgrade increases allowed crop tier and maximum capacity.
- 💊 **Med Station** — Healing zone when standing nearby. Upgrade reduces cooldown and increases HP restored per use.

**🔗 WARP TECH**

- 🔗 **Refinery Link** — Deposit items into the Refinery remotely. Reduces Backpack pressure when farming far from base.
- 🔗 **Turret Link** — Auto-loads dropped crops on designated tiles into the nearest Crop Turret. Upgrade increases tier & capacity.
- 🔗 **Market Link** — Sell items remotely — no need to return to the Farm Market. Convenient during daytime farming.

### 🏠 Farm House

Sleep to skip — skips the rest of the night. On wakeup: fully restores HP & Energy. Increases movement speed in 3h to 25%. Can sleep after clearing threats or from 3 AM.

> ⚠️ Sleeping early = losing night farming minutes (Granumz, crop, blueprint drops). Balance farming pace so Sleep is not spammed.

### 📦 Treasure Pod

Scattered at fixed locations on the map. Open to receive: 💰 Granumz · 🍎 Crops · ⚗️ Materials · 📋 Lv3 Blueprints

> Pod Blueprints require control skills and HandGun. Achievement **Pod Hunter**: open all → Granumz bonus + Vial Lv3 blueprint.
