01
Time Cycle
12'
☀️ Daytime
  • 💧Filter fresh water at the Water Purifier Machine for irrigation
  • 🌱Plant & harvest crops at the Garden
  • ⛏️Mine resources, explore the land and collect rewards from Treasure Pods
  • 🐔Manage the chicken coop, feed chickens and collect eggs at the Coop
  • ⚗️Process ingredients and food, craft Bottles at the Crafting Station
  • 🏗️Unlock Blueprints at the Builder's Shop
  • 🛠️Build structures via Fabricate Gadget
  • 🔧Upgrade and use all structures efficiently
8'
🌙 Nighttime
  • 👾Monsters spawn each night in waves starting at 7 PM
  • 🍅Defeat monsters using unusual ammo crafted from crops
  • 🧪Use Bottles to enhance combat
  • ⚠️Monsters can attack the player and eat chickens
  • 💎Drops on kill: Granumz, crops, blueprints, resources (stronger monsters = bigger rewards)
  • 💀Drops: Monster Meat → special ingredient
4'
💤 Sleep Phase
  • 😴Can sleep right after clearing each night's threat, or from 3 AM onward
  • ⚙️Machines and structures continue running automatically while the player sleeps
  • ⏳Sleeping early = losing night farming minutes. If you've optimized your time and got everything sorted on the farm — get some sleep. Why not?
Sleep Buff: Fully restore HP & Energy on wakeup. +33% movement speed in the morning (7–10 AM)

10
Science Lab

The Lab is the central tech hub — housing the Refinery, Fabricate Gadget, and Builder's Shop. This is where Gadgets are unlocked, crafted, and deployed onto the map.

🔬 Refinery

Central item storage vault. ⚠ Deposit only — items cannot be retrieved. Connect with Refinery Link to deposit remotely.

🏪 Builder's Shop

Unlock blueprints with Granumz and materials. Some special blueprints are only available from Treasure Pods (level 3 Gadgets). Unlocked blueprints are used in Fabricate Gadget.

🔧 Fabricate Gadget

Craft Gadgets from unlocked blueprints. Holds 1 Gadget at a time. Flow: Press Fabricate → move to map → select designated location → place.

💰 Farm Market

Sell crops and items for Granumz — the main currency. Dynamic pricing system: Perlin Noise + quantity-sold variance. Selling too much of one type → price drops. Diversify to maintain good prices. Connect Market Link to sell remotely.

# Upgrade Effect
1 Sell Value +5% (round 1) Blueprint purchased at Builder's Shop
2 Sell Value +5% (round 2) Total max +10% sell value (Unlock via Unlock Events)

🏠 Ranch House
