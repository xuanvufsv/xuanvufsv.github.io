☀️ Daytime

  • 😴Can sleep right after clearing each night's threat, or from 3 AM onward
  • ⚙️Machines and structures continue running automatically while the player sleeps
  • ⏳Sleeping early = losing night farming minutes. If you've optimized your time and got everything sorted on the farm — get some sleep. Why not?

Ingame Recipe Rules

  • Fixed Slots: Every recipe must consist of exactly 3 slots (3 sets of Item ×N).
  • Two-Ingredient Adjustment: If a recipe only has 2 ingredients (A×m + B×n), you must split one ingredient into two separate slots to reach a total of 3:
    • A×m + B×n → A×a + B×n + A×(m-a) (where 1 ≤ a < m)
  • Splitting Priority: Prioritize splitting the ingredient with the larger quantity or the one that is easier to divide.

Examples

  • Grape Jelly:
    🍇 Grape ×16 + 🍬 Sugar ×1 → 🍇 Grape ×8 + 🍬 Sugar ×1 + 🍇 Grape ×8
  • Oil Blend:
    🛢️ Bean Oil ×3 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 → 🛢️ Bean Oil ×2 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 + 🛢️ Bean Oil ×1
  • Alchemy Base:
    🧪 Vial Lv1 ×2 + 💎 Ruby ×2 → 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 + 💎 Ruby ×2 + 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 (or split the Ruby in the same manner)

Quy tắc công thức trong game

  • Ô cố định: Mỗi công thức gồm đúng 3 ô (3 bộ Vật phẩm ×N).
  • Điều chỉnh 2 nguyên liệu: Nếu công thức chỉ có 2 nguyên liệu (A×m + B×n), phải tách một nguyên liệu thành 2 ô riêng để đạt đủ 3 ô:
    • A×m + B×n → A×a + B×n + A×(m-a) (where 1 ≤ a < m)
  • Ưu tiên tách: Ưu tiên tách nguyên liệu có số lượng lớn hơn hoặc dễ chia hơn.

Ví dụ

  • Mứt Nho:
    🍇 Grape ×16 + 🍬 Sugar ×1 → 🍇 Grape ×8 + 🍬 Sugar ×1 + 🍇 Grape ×8
  • Hỗn hợp dầu:
    🛢️ Bean Oil ×3 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 → 🛢️ Bean Oil ×2 + 🧴 Coconut Oil ×1 + 🛢️ Bean Oil ×1
  • Chất gốc giả kim:
    🧪 Vial Lv1 ×2 + 💎 Ruby ×2 → 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 + 💎 Ruby ×2 + 🧪 Vial Lv1 ×1 (hoặc tách Ruby theo cách tương tự)
